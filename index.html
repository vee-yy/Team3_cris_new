Document Requirements

Requirements for Birth Certificate Registration

  • Parents' Marriage Certificate (if parents are married)
  • Valid IDs of parents (at least two government-issued IDs)
  • Affidavit of Acknowledgement/Admission of Paternity (for illegitimate children)
  • Affidavit of Delayed Registration (if registered after 30 days from birth)

Note: Additional documents may be required for special cases like foreign parentage or foundlings.

Requirements for Marriage Certificate Registration

  • Marriage License (issued by the local civil registrar)
  • Valid IDs of both spouses (at least two government-issued IDs each)
  • Certificate of Attendance from pre-marriage counseling seminar
  • Affidavit of Delayed Registration (if registered beyond 15 days from marriage)

Note: For foreign marriages, additional authentication may be required.

Requirements for Death Certificate Registration

  • Funeral Permit
  • Valid ID of the informant (usually next of kin)
  • Affidavit of Delayed Registration (if registered beyond 30 days from death)
  • Medical Certificate (stating cause of death)

Note: For deaths without medical attendance, additional requirements may apply.

Requirements for CENOMAR Request

  • Valid ID (government-issued with photo)
  • Notarized Application Form
  • Authorization Letter (if requested by another person)
  • Payment Receipt (official receipt of payment)

Note: CENOMAR (Certificate of No Marriage) is typically required for marriage license applications.

Requirements for CENODEATH Request

  • Valid ID (government-issued with photo)
  • Proof of Relationship (if requesting for a relative)
  • Notarized Application Form
  • Authorization Letter (if requested by another person)
  • Payment Receipt (official receipt of payment)

Note: CENODEATH (Certificate of No Death Record) is often required for legal or inheritance purposes.

Important: All documents must be original or certified true copies. Photocopies must be presented with original documents for verification.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What certificates can I request?

Birth, Marriage, Death, Cenomar, and Cenodeath certificates issued by the Philippine government. For other certificate kindly contact our support.

How can I submit and download the Request for Correction (R.A. 9048) form?

For walk-in Application. Kindly, submit the form and select Document Correction from there you can submit and download the form you will be also given an Appointment ID.

Can I register for a family member?

Yes, with their written authorization and your valid ID.

What if I don't have all the documents?

Provide available IDs and explain gaps in the "Remarks" section.

Is my data secure?

Yes! We use encryption and comply with the Data Privacy Act.

What's the fee for certificates?

Fees vary by document type. The system will display the amount before payment.

What if my payment fails?

Retry or contact your bank. For issues, email support@civilregistry.gov.ph.

How do I correct a form?

Edit details in your dashboard before payment. After submission, contact support.

Where's my certificate?

Log in to track status. Delays may occur during holidays.
